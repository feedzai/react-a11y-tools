# Skip Links

<Subtitle>
	Provide ways for users to skip to important sections of our websites. It helps users using
	screen-readers to navigate easier and more efficiently.
</Subtitle>

## How to operate this demo

- Press the `tab` key
- A skip link will be shown on the top of the demo.
- Press `enter`
- Focus will be set on the area of that `SkipLink`.
